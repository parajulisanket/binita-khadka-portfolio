About Binita

Binita Khadka represents a modern, accountable, and people-focused leadership model built on transparency and measurable progress.

Her approach centers on listening first, engaging directly with communities, and implementing solutions that improve everyday life — strengthening institutions, expanding opportunities for youth, and ensuring long-term sustainability.

This portfolio reflects her mission for 2026: to build trust, empower communities, and deliver practical, transparent governance.
