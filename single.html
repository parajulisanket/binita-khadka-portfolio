Community Listening Program Launch.

Published on October 24, 2026

A new initiative has been officially introduced to transform how local governance interacts with residents. This comprehensive program is designed specifically to collect direct citizen feedback, ensuring that grassroots opinions directly shape local development strategies.

By implementing direct feedback loops, the framework prioritizes immediate local needs across every single ward, establishing a new benchmark for municipal accountability.

The program introduces a series of structured town halls and digital submission portals. These platforms ensure complete transparency, allowing community members to monitor exactly how their inputs impact final budgetary choices and public project scheduling.

Through this deep-rooted accountability system, Binita Khadka emphasizes a commitment to stronger community collaboration, bringing public servants and everyday citizens together for long-term regional progress.
